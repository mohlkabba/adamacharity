Our Mission

Built on compassion.
Driven by community.

Adama's Relief & Rehabilitation Foundation provides humanitarian relief and long-term rehabilitation support to vulnerable people — including widows, the elderly, and families with disabilities.

Our Ramadan 2026 campaign reached over 50 families on 27th February 2026, delivering essential food parcels with the help of our dedicated volunteer community.

"Every parcel we deliver is a message: you are not forgotten."
— Adama's Relief & Rehabilitation Foundation
