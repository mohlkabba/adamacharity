Food Distribution

Food Relief & Emergency Aid

Our flagship programme delivers essential food parcels to widows, elderly individuals, and families with disabilities. Running during Ramadan and in response to emergencies, we ensure that the most vulnerable in our community are never left hungry.

Our first major distribution — 27th February 2026 — reached over 50 families. Every parcel contains essential staple foods, selected to meet the needs of diverse households.

  • Ramadan annual food distribution campaign
  • Emergency response food parcels
  • Direct delivery to households — no middlemen
  • Focus on widows, elderly, and disabled families
  • Volunteer-packed and volunteer-delivered

Rehabilitation Support

Assistive Equipment & Long-term Support

Beyond food, we recognise that many in our community need practical support to live independently. Our rehabilitation programme provides assistive equipment — walking aids, wheelchairs, and mobility support — to disabled individuals who cannot access or afford them.

We believe that rehabilitation is not just physical — it is about restoring confidence, dignity, and the ability to participate fully in community life.

  • Wheelchairs and mobility aids for disabled individuals
  • Walking frames and assistive devices
  • Long-term follow-up and support visits
  • Partnership with local health workers
  • Priority given to those with no other support network

Community Support

Outreach, Clothing & Welfare

Our community support programme goes beyond food and equipment. We run clothing drives, welfare visits, and outreach events that connect isolated individuals with the wider community — because no one should feel invisible.

We believe in building a community where everyone looks out for one another. Our outreach volunteers regularly visit elderly and disabled residents to check on their wellbeing, offer companionship, and connect them with available resources.

  • Seasonal clothing drives for families in need
  • Welfare check visits to elderly and isolated individuals
  • Community events and gatherings
  • Referrals to local health and social services
  • Volunteer-led outreach and companionship

The Process

How we deliver aid

From donation to doorstep — here is how every contribution reaches the people who need it most.

1

Community identifies need

Families, volunteers, and community leaders identify households in need of support.

2

Donations fund supplies

Your donations are used to purchase essential food parcels, equipment, and supplies.

3

Volunteers pack & prepare

Our volunteer team packs every parcel carefully and prepares for distribution day.

4

Direct delivery to families

Parcels are delivered directly to families — no middlemen, no delays, 100% reach.
