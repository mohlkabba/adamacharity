Our Ramadan 2026 Food Distribution campaign is our most ambitious yet. We are working to support 50–100 families across our community — with a focus on those who are most overlooked: widows raising children alone, elderly individuals with no family support, and households where disability means earning a living is impossible.

Our first distribution event on 27th February 2026 successfully reached over 50 families. We are continuing to raise funds to reach the remaining households before the end of Ramadan season.

Who we're serving

Widows

Women raising families alone, with limited income and no financial support.

Elderly

Older residents living alone, unable to work, and without regular access to food.

Disabled Families

Households where disability limits earning potential and access to resources.
